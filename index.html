About CHAN

CHAN is a biennial football tournament organized by CAF, featuring national teams made up of players who play in their country's domestic leagues.

Host Country

Uganda will proudly host CHAN 2025, showcasing its vibrant culture and passion for football.

Key Dates

The tournament kicks off in August 2025.

Top Contenders

Expect fierce competition from Morocco, Tanzania, Kenya, and the host nation Uganda.

Upcoming Matches

  • Uganda vs. Kenya
  • Tanzania vs. Morocco
